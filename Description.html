OTHER DETAILS

Combining peerless refinement with capability and power, the 557 HP V8 Supercharged engine has been enhanced to deliver maximum torque.

Contemporary design is complemented by the very best in British craftsmanship. The cabin features the finest Diamond Quilted leathers and a Knurled finish to the Stop/Start button and foot pedals. The rotary shifter, and Steel Weave Carbon Fiber front and rear door veneers reflect the distinctive SVAutobiography Dynamic personality.

The SVAutobiography Dynamic features 21" 5 Split-Spoke ‘Style 5005’ with Dark Grey finish as standard. 22" 5 Split-Spoke ‘Style 5087’ with Diamond Turned finish and Dark Grey contrast are an option.

The unique suspension configuration on the SVAutobiography Dynamic has been calibrated to match the increase to 557 HP power output.

Exclusive SVAutobiography styling includes a range of unique Graphite Atlas features including Graphite Atlas front grille and Graphite Atlas side vent, both with Bright Chrome inserts.

The very finest Diamond Quilted seats offer 24-way heated and cooled, 'Hot-Stone' massage front seats with Executive Class Comfort-Plus rear seats. Available in a variety of interior colorways.

The sense of refinement is heightened by touches such as the Knurled finish to the Stop/Start button and foot pedals, together with the Steel Weave Carbon Fiber trim finishers.
